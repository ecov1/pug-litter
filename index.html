About the Puppies

Winston and Lulu were both pandemic puppies born in January 2020 in California. They moved to New York when they were 9 months old and soon after, they became parents for the first time! These puppies are their third and last litter.



They were conceived on Valentine’s Day 2025 when Lulu hopped the big metal cage barrier in the stairwell to join her mate. 2 puppies were born late on the evening of Good Friday and 5 puppies were born a few hours into Saturday.

There are 5 male and 2 female. I will be holding on to the little black male for 6 months because it is too sad to see everyone leave - for the puppy parents and for us.
